About GymLAB

Hi! We're a chain of multipurpose gyms with locations in Karpenisi, Athens and Patras.

GymLAB was started by Snorty and Papi in 1999. It's our mission to help people in our hometowns get fit, stay healthy and live life on their terms without fitness being an obstacle.
